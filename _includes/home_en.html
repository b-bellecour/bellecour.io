Glad to meet you!

I am Baptiste BELLECOUR, a trilingual information security engineer originally from Paris, i currently work in Tokyo, Japan. I became fascinated by the world of information technology at 10 years old, through an Atari Falcon030. Since that day, i knew that computing will have a big role in my life.

After being successfull in various IT fields such as networking, systems administration and softwares developement, i am now employed as an information security engineer.

From my point of view, information security is a continuous evolution domain where learning is never ending, and this is precisely what I love.
